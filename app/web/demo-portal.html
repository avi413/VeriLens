What VeriLens Does

Problem: How do you prove a photo is real in the age of AI and deepfakes?
Solution: VeriLens creates an unbreakable cryptographic "fingerprint" for photos at capture time, stored permanently on blockchain. Any tampering changes the fingerprint, making fraud instantly detectable.
